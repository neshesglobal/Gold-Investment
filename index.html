Risk Factors & Mitigants
This programme is structured specifically to minimise investor risk at every layer. The following table is provided for disclosure purposes and reflects our active mitigation strategy.
Risk Factor Likelihood Mitigation in Place
Gold Price Volatility
 Low
Returns are fixed at 9% p.a., entirely independent of spot price movements. The investor is a secured lender — not a market participant. Gold is the collateral, not the exposure.
Counterparty / Buyer Default
 Low
All buyers are contracted under 12-month forward agreements with prior credit assessments. Physical gold provides a natural settlement backstop — in any default, gold is already vaulted and ring-fenced.
Custody & Theft Risk
 Very Low
All gold is held with Brinks in segregated, fully insured allocated storage in UAE. Inventory is independently reconciled. Investors have audit rights over their pledged gold position at all times.
Regulatory & Legal Risk
 Low
Operations structured under IFZA free zone law with full AML/KYC compliance. All legal documentation is prepared by qualified UAE counsel. Investor protection is codified via pledge and loan note agreements.
